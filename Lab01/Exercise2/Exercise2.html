Our Mission

San Joaquin Valley Town Hall is a non-profit organization that is run by an all-volunteer board of directors. Our mission is to bring nationally and internationally renowned, thought-provoking speakers who inform, educate, and entertain our audience! As one of our members told us:


"Each year I give a ticket package to each of our family members. I think of it as the gift of knowledge...and that is priceless."

Speaker of the Month

Fossil Threads in the Web of Life

February
Scott Sampson

What's 75 million years old and brand spank'in new? A teenage Triceratops! Come to the California Academy of Sciences with Scott Sampson, Research Curator at the Utah Museum of Natural History, to see for yourself the fascinating mix of ancient animals and young dinosaurs. Sampson's research has focused on the ecology and evolution of late Cretaceous dinosaurs and he has conducted fieldwork in a number of countries in Africa.
